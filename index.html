Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to deploy an AI agent?

Typically, we can have a custom AI agent trained and deployed within 10-14 days depending on the complexity of your business data.

Will the AI hallucinate or give wrong info?

We use "Knowledge Base Guardrails." The AI is strictly limited to your provided documentation and FAQs. If it doesn't know an answer, it's programmed to hand over to a human.

Do you build mobile apps?

We specialize in Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) which work perfectly on mobile devices without the need for App Store downloads, saving you time and cost.

What kind of support do you offer post-launch?

We provide monthly maintenance retainers where we update the AI model, refine responses based on chat logs, and ensure all integrations are running smoothly.
